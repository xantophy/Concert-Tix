Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ticket purchases, events, and more.

To purchase tickets:

  1. Browse events on our website or app
  2. Select your desired event and click "Buy Tickets"
  3. Choose your seats or ticket type
  4. Proceed to checkout and enter payment details
  5. Receive your e-tickets via email

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, and digital wallets including GoPay, OVO, and DANA. Bank transfers are also available for some events.

Most tickets are delivered electronically via email as PDF attachments or mobile tickets. You'll receive them immediately after purchase. For some events, tickets may be available for mobile entry only through our app.

Yes, for most events you can present your ticket barcode on your smartphone screen for scanning at the venue. Ensure your phone is charged and brightness is turned up. Some venues may require printed tickets - check your ticket instructions.

All ticket sales are typically final. Exchanges or refunds may be available only if:

  • The event is canceled
  • The event is rescheduled and you can't attend the new date
  • Required by local consumer laws

Please see our Refund Policy for complete details.

Postponed events: Your tickets will typically be valid for the new date. If you can't attend, refunds may be available during a limited window.

Canceled events: You'll automatically receive a refund to your original payment method within 14 business days.

We'll notify you by email if there are any changes to your event.

We recommend:

  • Following the event organizer's social media
  • Checking our website for updates
  • Ensuring your contact information is current in your account
  • Subscribing to our newsletter for important announcements

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter the email associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Follow the instructions to create a new password

*You can also change password by accessing profile menu
