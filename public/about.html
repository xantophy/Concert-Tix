Our Story

At ConcertTix, we believe that live music has the power to inspire, unite, and create lifelong memories.

Our Mission

Founded in 2025, our mission is to make it easy for music fans to find and purchase tickets to the best concerts, festivals, and music events across Indonesia.

ConcertTix was born from our love of music and technology. As concert-goers ourselves, we understand the frustration of complicated ticket purchasing processes and untrustworthy ticket sellers.

That's why we created ConcertTix—a trusted, transparent, fan-first platform to ensure everyone can enjoy live music experiences with ease.
